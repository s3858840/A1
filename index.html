Interest in IT

My interest in IT has started from childhood by assembling and repairing my desktop. I have wondered by seeing what a computer can do. And now it has become a core part in every field. All the systems are working over network. For example, BMS, Powergrid, AV, Medical etc. I have started my career in IT and have reached in AV. But now everything in AV is run through IT. Like the system programming, data transfer over network etc. During the last year in my career I have been influenced by the Director of IT and he has guided me to the world of IT Leadership. I have started preparing for ITIL and my interest it to be in a leadership role that involves IT.
